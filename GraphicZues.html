Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

What is your design process?

Our process involves four key stages: Discovery (understanding your needs), Design (creating concepts), Development (refining the design), and Delivery (providing final assets). We collaborate with you at every step.

How much does a project cost?

Project costs vary based on scope, complexity, and deliverables. We provide a detailed, custom quote after our initial discovery call. Please get in touch for a free estimate!

How long will my project take?

Timelines depend on the project. A simple logo might take 1-2 weeks, while a full website design can take 4-8 weeks. We'll provide a clear timeline in our project proposal.

What file formats will I receive?

You'll receive all standard file formats you need. For logos, this includes vector files (AI, EPS, SVG) and raster files (PNG, JPG). For web assets, we provide optimized formats for digital use.

Do you offer revisions?

Absolutely. Our process includes rounds of revisions (typically 2-3) at key stages. We want to ensure you are 100% happy with the final design, and your feedback is a crucial part of the process.
